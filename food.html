DISH OF THE DAY

Bulalo

This is Bulalo, one of the most beloved foods here in the Philippines. It's a bone-marrow and beef shank stew dish that includes corn and other types of leafy veggies. It is similar to another Filipino dish called Nilaga, but meat chunks are used in Nilaga rather than bone-marrows and beef shanks. You can even slurp the bone marrow from the beef shank as that is how it's usually eaten. It's very fun to eat and is quaranteed to satisfy your hunger!

Top Food Picks

Charlie Chan Pasta

Charlie Chan Chicken Pasta is a dish from Yellow Cab. It is a spicy, somewhat sweet noodle dish combined with chicken, mushrooms, onions and nuts. It goes perfectly well with pizza that Yellow Cab also serves. I have eaten this pasta a lot of times now and it will always be my favorite. If you ever stop by and eat at a Yellow Cab, try it out!

Lechong Manok

Lechong Manok is a dish that's usually served in karinderias or ihawans. It can also be found at Kenny Rogers Roasters. A big metal stick is speared into the chicken and is then put over fire. This will roast the chicken, of course. It kind of is like how Lechong Baboy is made, but this time it's a chicken.

Red Velvet Cake

Red Velvet Cake is a delicious, sweet, and red cake. Red velvet may taste a little sour or salty at times since we do use buttermilk in the recipe. It is soft, moist, buttery, and as I have said before, very tasty! Not to mention it is also the name of one of my favorite K-pop Groups. If you're looking to have fun baking and eating with your family, bake a red velvet cake!
